Budget classes

Low-cost 💰

Low-cost category ensures that Vaivers can enjoy an economy/budget trip at the minimum cost possible, but in the meantime, ensuring maximum comfort. Choosing the Low-cost budget category will entail the following:

1. The accommodation will always be a 3-star hotel (with the opportunity to upgrade later).

2. No worries! It will always be located the closest possible to the city center.

3. All flights will be operated by Low-cost Airlines (Vueling, Ryanair, ...)

4. The purchased tickets will not include Seat Allocation, Cabin Bag or Extra Luggage included. You will be able to do the check-in on your own, and choose upgrades at your own expense.

5. Rest assured, you will receive an exclusive travel guide for you trip based on your preferences, your budget, and the dates you will be visiting the designated country.


Mid-cost 💵

The Mid-cost category ensures that Vaivers get the greatest comfort for the trip and choosing this category entails the following specifications:

1. The accommodation will always be a 4-star or 5-star hotel.

2. The hotel will always be located in the city center.

3. The flights might be operated by either Low-cost Airlines or High-tier Airlines.

4. If the flights are operated by a Low-cost Airline, the price will include Seat Allocation (5 first rows) and the option to include Cabin Bag or Extra Luggage. You will still be able to check-in yourself and customize according to the needs.

5. Rest assured, you will receive an exclusive travel guide for your trip based on your budget preferences and the dates you will be visiting the designated country, including best non-touristic restaurants, best way to commute from the airport to the hotel and back and other tips!


Luxury 💎

Are you ready for the top-notch Vaiving experience? This is a category only for the ones who can afford a top-notch & luxurious traveling experience. You want to book a luxury flight, stress-free and quick, and we will do it for you. This budget category will entail the following:

1. The accommodation will always be a 5-star (or 5-star +) hotel with an option to upgrade to different suite sizes and categories.

2. The hotel will always be located in the city center. For commuting from and to the airport, a driver will be waiting upon your arrival, with the option of keeping the driver for all the trip at extra cost.

3. The flights will be operated by High-tier Airlines, with the option to upgrade to business/first-class at extra cost.

4. Further customization to your trip will be available: driver to visit the city, security, and [insert other luxury stuff].

5. You will get an exclusively made guide for your specific trip, based on your preferences and your budget: best places to dine and best places to visit, including a 24/7 customer service support line in case any questions arise during your trip to the designated country. 

About the Airports

From Schengen Area

We are happy to accept customers from all over the Schengen Area. This means that flying from will be available for all the countries in the Schengen Area, although the countries where we fly to might be limited at this moment. The countries in the Schengen Area are the following:

Germany, Austria, Belgium, Croatia, Denmark, Spain, Slovakia, Slovenia, Estonia, Finland, France, Greece, Holland, Hungary, Iceland (non-EU), Italy, Latvia, Liechtenstein (non-EU), Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Norway (non-EU), Poland, Portugal, Czech Republic, Sweden and Switzerland (non-EU).

From Outside the Schengen Area

We are also happy to accept customers flying from outside the Schengen Area. At this moment, we only operate in the European market where we are happy to offer our services to customers living in the following countries:

Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Ireland and the United Kingdom.

From the rest of the world

As much as we would like to offer our services in all the countries in the world, the only countries we are currently operating in are the above. But hey, we might be coming to you soon! As we expand internationally, we will announce it in our Social Media, so stay tuned and make sure to follow us in Instagram and LinkedIn for updates!
